2Brothers Sports

This is the official homepage of 2Brothers Sports! 2BS is recorded live every Friday at 5pm central.

Skastaa and Captainhuff are considering taking their hobby up a notch and starting to blog, but they dont have a website.

Skastaa wants to keep the website simple, a homepage, a page for Captainhuff, and a contact page.

Captainhuff just wants a place where he can write his own thoughts down in long form, instead of wading through the slop that is twitter.

Their "fans" end up using their phone a lot and need a respoonsive site.

Problem Statement

2Brothers Sports is looking to expand on their once a week sports show. Both Skastaa and Captainhuff want a place where they can share their thoughts in long form, while also keeping an archive of everything. Twitch doesn't have written form and Twitter is too short. This new website will be the future home of all things 2Brothers Sports, a spot where 2BS can really be themselves.
